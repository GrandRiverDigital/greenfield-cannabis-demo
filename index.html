Who We Are

Kitchener's Community-First
Cannabis Retailer

Greenfield Cannabis Co. is an independently owned and operated dispensary in the heart of Kitchener. We opened because we believed local cannabis retail should look, feel, and operate differently — with more knowledge, more care, and less corporate.

Every staff member goes through in-depth product training. We ask questions, we listen, and we match people with what actually works for them — whether you're a first-timer or a long-time connoisseur.
