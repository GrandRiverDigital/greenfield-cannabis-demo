Our Story

A Dispensary Built for
the Community

Greenfield Cannabis Co. opened in 2021 with a single mission: bring a premium, welcoming cannabis retail experience to the Waterloo Region. We were tired of seeing customers feel judged, ignored, or overwhelmed — so we built the opposite.

Our store is clean, bright, and approachable. Our staff aren't just passionate — they're trained. Every team member goes through comprehensive product education so they can help you find exactly what you're looking for, whether you're a daily consumer or exploring cannabis for the first time.

We're locally owned, fully licensed by the AGCO, and committed to responsible, community-focused retail. No pressure. No judgment. Just excellent cannabis and honest advice.

Why Greenfield

We Do This Differently

Not every dispensary is the same. Here's what makes us the go-to for the Waterloo Region.

AGCO Compliant & Fully Licensed

We operate under full AGCO licensing and compliance. Every product we carry is legal, lab-tested, and sourced from licensed Canadian producers. You can buy with complete confidence.

Staff Who Actually Know Their Stuff

Every team member completes extensive cannabinoid education and product training. First visit or hundredth — we'll help you find the right product for your experience level and goal.

Quality You Can Count On

We don't just stock everything — we curate. Our team samples and vets products before they hit our shelves. If we wouldn't recommend it to our own family, we don't carry it.

Community-First Retail

We're locally owned, locally operated, and deeply invested in the Waterloo Region. We support local producers, participate in community events, and believe responsible cannabis retail makes our city better.

Private & Judgment-Free

Whether you're a first-time buyer or a long-time consumer, our store is a judgment-free zone. Discreet, respectful service for every customer, every time. Your privacy matters to us.

Extended Hours, Convenient Location

Open Monday through Saturday 10am–9pm and Sunday 11am–6pm. Downtown Kitchener location with easy parking and transit access. We're here when it's convenient for you.
