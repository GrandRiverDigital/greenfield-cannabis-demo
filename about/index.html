Who We Are

Kitchener's Cannabis Store,
Built Different

Greenfield Cannabis Co. is an independently owned and operated dispensary in the heart of Kitchener. We opened because we believed local cannabis retail should look, feel, and operate differently — with more knowledge, more care, and less corporate.

Every staff member goes through in-depth product training. We ask questions, we listen, and we match people with what actually works for them — whether you're a first-timer or a long-time connoisseur.

Expert, judgment-free guidance. Whether you're brand new or deeply experienced — we meet you where you are, no pressure, no condescension.

Ontario-first curation. We prioritize Ontario-grown and small-batch producers wherever possible. We believe local quality deserves local shelf space.

Private and discreet. Your visit is your business. We maintain a welcoming, professional environment where everyone feels comfortable.
